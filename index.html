WHAT'S HAPPENING

Animals are humanely killed usually by a lethal injection when they have been there for a certain time or the shelter has run out of space. There are also no kill shelters, but these can end up turning many dogs away to far worse fates since they end up full of unadoptable animals.

Adopt, dont shop shelter pets have every bit as much love to give as one from a breeder. If you do none of these things, then do this at least look closely at those shelter workers and think about the pain they willingly take on every day because someone else chose not to hold up their end of the bargain.

STATISTICS:


About six million animals enter shelters every year. Approximately 32% of animals are killed ( 22% of dogs and 45% of cats).

Less than 50% of shelter animals are irremediably suffering.

Over one million people live in communities saving between 98% and 99% of dogs and cats in their shelters (over two million saving at least 98% of dogs).

Over 10 million people live in communities saving between 90% and 99% of dogs and cats in their shelters.

Over 50 million people live in communities saving at least 80% of dogs and cats in their shelters.
